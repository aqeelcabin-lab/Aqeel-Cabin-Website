Frequently asked questions

What are check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in is 3:00 PM and check-out is 12:00 noon. Times may be flexible when there is no reservation immediately before or after your stay.

How do I check availability?

Use the direct-booking assistant. Select Cabin or Dome, enter exact arrival and departure dates, and submit your request.

Is the price per person?

No. The listed nightly price is for the entire Cabin or the entire Dome, subject to the stated guest limit.

Is breakfast included?

No. Breakfast may be arranged in advance for approximately US$10 per person and delivered to the accommodation.

Are pets allowed?

Pets may be accepted depending on size and behaviour. The Dome normally allows only one small, well-behaved dog. Pets are not allowed on beds or sofas.

Is there Wi-Fi?

Yes. High-speed satellite internet is available at the Cabin, with service extended to the Dome. Rainforest conditions and power interruptions can occasionally affect service.

Can I arrive without meeting anyone?

Yes. Both stays normally use self check-in. After confirmation, you receive the driving video, parking details and private door-access information.

Can I use the river and trails?

Yes, subject to weather and river conditions. These are natural areas used at your own risk. Children require close supervision.

Are monkeys, sloths or toucans guaranteed?

No. They live freely in the surrounding habitat. Sightings are possible but never guaranteed.

How do direct payments work?

Accepted methods are Yappy/Banco General, PayPal and approved cash arrangements. Direct Visa and Mastercard payments are not currently available.
